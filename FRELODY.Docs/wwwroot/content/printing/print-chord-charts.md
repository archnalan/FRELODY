# Print chord charts

> Print a song's chord chart straight from FRELODY using your device's print dialog.

## Overview

Sometimes you just want the chords on paper — a sheet on the music stand instead of a screen. With Premium, FRELODY can print a song's chord chart directly from the share menu.

Printing is a Premium feature. If you're on the free plan, you can read more on [Plans and billing](/docs/pricing/plans). You print from a song you're viewing, using your device's own print dialog, so you can pick a printer or save to paper as usual.

## Steps

1. Open the song you want from your library.
2. Open the share menu (the **Share** dropdown on the song).
3. Choose **Print**.
4. FRELODY opens your device's print dialog and shows "Print dialog opened!".
5. Pick your printer and settings, then print.

## Tips

- If you see "Print Unavailable — Printing is not available on this device", your current device or browser doesn't support direct printing. Try a desktop browser, or use [Export songs as PDF](/docs/printing/export-pdf) instead.
- Use your print dialog's "Save as PDF" destination if you'd rather keep a file than print on paper.
- Check the print preview so the chart fits the page before you commit ink.
