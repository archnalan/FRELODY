# Today's Songs

> Everything you've unlocked today, gathered in one place and free to replay for 24 hours.

## Overview

**Today's Songs** is your shortlist for the day. Every song you analyse is added here, and stays playable for 24 hours, so you can pick up where you left off without starting a fresh analysis.

You'll find it at `/discover/today`, with the subtitle "Songs you unlocked today — available for 24 hours." It's a signed-in page, so if you're not signed in FRELODY sends you to sign in first. For that, see [Sign in and sign out](/docs/getting-started/sign-in-and-sign-out).

The page shows a grid of the songs you've unlocked, each with its thumbnail, a platform icon, the title, and how long it has left, such as "Nh left" or "Nm left", or "Expired" once the window closes. Tap one to replay it: YouTube songs reopen their analysis page, and TikTok songs reopen with their original link. If you haven't analysed anything yet, you'll see "No songs yet today" and a **Discover a song** button to get started.

Today's Songs pairs naturally with **Premium**. With unlimited analyses you can fill this list freely and keep every song to hand all day. To see what Premium includes without the hard sell, read [Plans and billing](/docs/pricing/plans).

## Tips

- The "Nh left" / "Nm left" label counts down the 24-hour replay window for each song.
- Replaying a song from here doesn't use another free analysis; it's already unlocked.
- Empty list? Use **Discover a song** to analyse your first song of the day.
