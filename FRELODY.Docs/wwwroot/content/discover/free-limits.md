# Free daily limits and song length

> What you get for free each day: how many songs, how long they can be, and how long they stay playable.

## Overview

FRELODY is free to try, with no card to start. On the **Starter (free)** tier you can analyse 2 songs every day. That's plenty to learn something new, and the count resets at midnight UTC.

Each free analysis counts once. FRELODY tracks distinct songs you unlock, so re-opening the same song doesn't cost you another slot. Songs that are already in the FRELODY library don't count towards your daily limit at all; only new YouTube or TikTok analyses do.

There are also length caps. Free songs can be up to 8 minutes long. Premium raises that to 20 minutes. A song longer than the premium cap is marked "too long to analyze", and signing in or upgrading won't change that, it's simply over the limit.

Once you've analysed a song, it stays free to replay for a 24-hour window, so you can keep practising it through the day without spending another slot. You'll find those songs in [Today's Songs](/docs/discover/todays-songs).

## Limits

- **Analyses per day:** 2 on free, reset at midnight UTC.
- **Song length:** up to 8 minutes free, up to 20 minutes on Premium.
- **Replay window:** anything you unlock stays free to play for 24 hours.
- **Library songs:** songs already in FRELODY never count against your daily limit.

## What you'll see

When you reach a limit, FRELODY tells you why. If you're not signed in, it nudges you with "Sign in to play your first free song" and reminds you that there are "2 free song analyses every day". Once you've used your free songs, it shows "You've used today's free songs", an option to **View today's songs**, an **Upgrade to keep playing** prompt, and a **Maybe later** dismissal.

To lift the daily cap and get the longer length limit, see [Unlimited analysis (Premium)](/docs/discover/unlimited-analysis).
