# Paste a song link

> Paste a YouTube or TikTok link, or search by name, to start an analysis.

## Overview

The fastest way into FRELODY is to paste a link. Drop a YouTube or TikTok song into the **Discover** search box and FRELODY takes it from there, detecting the chords and lining them up with the lyrics so you can play along.

The single search field is flexible. On YouTube it accepts a song title or artist for a search, or a full YouTube URL. If you paste a `tiktok.com` link, FRELODY spots it and hands it to the TikTok lane automatically, whichever source toggle you have selected. So you never have to switch tabs first.

This page is for everyone, signed in or not. You can try analysis straight away within the free daily limit. To see how many free songs you get and the length caps, read [Free daily limits and song length](/docs/discover/free-limits).

## Steps

1. Open **Discover** at `/discover`, or start from the home page.
2. Choose your source with the **YouTube** / **TikTok** toggle if you want, though a pasted TikTok link routes itself.
3. Paste your link into the search box, or type a song title or artist for a YouTube search.
4. Press **Enter** or tap the search button. Submit stays disabled until you've typed something.
5. Pick your song from the results to open its analysis page.

## Tips

- If a TikTok link isn't recognised, you'll see "Please paste a valid TikTok video link." Check you copied the full `tiktok.com/@user/video/…` URL.
- If a YouTube search fails, FRELODY shows "Search failed. Please try again." with a **Retry** button.
