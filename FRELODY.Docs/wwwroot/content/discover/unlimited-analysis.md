# Unlimited analysis (Premium)

> With Premium there's no daily cap, and you can analyse full-length songs up to 20 minutes.

## Overview

The free tier gives you 2 analyses a day, which is enough to dip in. But if you're learning several songs, or working through longer tracks, the daily cap and the 8-minute length limit start to get in the way. That's what **Premium** removes.

With Premium you get unlimited song analyses, with no daily cap. Analyse as many YouTube and TikTok songs as you like, whenever the inspiration strikes. The free limit simply no longer applies.

Premium also raises the length cap. Free songs can be up to 8 minutes; Premium lets you analyse full-length songs up to 20 minutes. That covers most tracks end to end, so you're no longer cut off partway through a longer song.

Premium is granted as soon as your payment is confirmed, and it stays active for as long as your plan runs. To understand how access is applied and when it renews or lapses, see [How premium access works](/docs/pricing/how-premium-works). For prices and what each plan includes, see [Plans and billing](/docs/pricing/plans).

## What Premium changes

- **No daily cap:** the 2-per-day free limit is lifted entirely.
- **Longer songs:** the length cap rises from 8 minutes to 20 minutes.
- **Same loop:** everything else works exactly as it does on the free tier, just without the limits.
