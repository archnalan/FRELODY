# Settings

> Set your theme, notifications and how songs are displayed, all from the Settings tab on your profile.

## Overview

The **Settings** tab on your profile controls how FRELODY looks and behaves for you. Open your profile at `/user-profile` and select **Settings**.

Under **Appearance** you will find a **Theme** dropdown and a **Notify** switch. The **Theme** setting is per-account, so your choice of light or dark travels with you. (The theme toggle in the navigation bar sets the same look for the current browser.)

Under **Display** you can choose a song **Mode** with a segmented control: **Full** (lyrics, chords and chord charts together), lyrics-and-chords, or lyrics-only. This is the layout FRELODY uses when you view a song.

## Steps

1. Open `/user-profile` and select the **Settings** tab.
2. Under **Appearance**, choose your preferred **Theme** from the dropdown.
3. Toggle the **Notify** switch to set your notification preference.
4. Under **Display**, pick a song **Mode**: **Full**, lyrics-and-chords, or lyrics-only.
5. Your choices apply to how songs appear when you view them.

## Tips

- The **Theme** setting is tied to your account; the navigation-bar toggle only affects the current browser.
- Choose lyrics-only **Mode** for a cleaner sing-along view when you do not need the chords on screen.
