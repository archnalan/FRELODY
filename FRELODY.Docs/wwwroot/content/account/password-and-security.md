# Password and security

> Change your password and see what security options FRELODY offers from the Account tab on your profile.

## Overview

Your security settings live under the **Account** tab on your profile. Open your profile at `/user-profile`, select **Account**, then look under **Security**.

The **Security** section has a **Password** entry — "Change your account password" — which opens the **Change Password** dialog. There is also a **Two-Factor Authentication** option, "Add an extra layer of security", which is marked **Coming Soon** and is not yet available.

If you have forgotten your password, FRELODY can email you a reset link. Following that link takes you to a reset page where you can set a new password. In some cases FRELODY may also require you to change your password on your next sign-in.

## Steps

1. Open `/user-profile` and select the **Account** tab.
2. Under **Security**, select **Password** to open the **Change Password** dialog.
3. Enter your current password and your new password as prompted.
4. Confirm to save the new password.

## Tips

- If you cannot sign in, use the password-reset link emailed to you and follow it to set a new password.
- **Two-Factor Authentication** is listed as **Coming Soon** and is not yet active.
- Choose a strong, unique password you do not use on other sites.
