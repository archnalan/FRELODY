# Your profile

> Find your profile, edit your details and move between your library, recovery, settings and account tabs.

## Overview

Your profile is your home base in FRELODY. It is where your songs live, where your account details are kept, and where you can change how the app looks and behaves. Open it at `/user-profile` once you are signed in.

The profile page has a header showing your details, plus a set of tabs: **Library**, **Recovery**, **Settings** and **Account**. The header has a view mode and an edit mode, so you can update your name and details without leaving the page.

Use this page when you want to check what you have saved, restore unsaved work, change your theme, or update your password.

## Steps

1. Open `/user-profile` (or select your name or avatar in the navigation).
2. Read your current details in the profile header.
3. Select the edit control in the header to switch to **Edit profile**.
4. Update your full name and any other details shown.
5. Select **Save** to keep your changes, or **Cancel** to discard them.
6. Use the **Library**, **Recovery**, **Settings** and **Account** tabs to manage the rest of your profile.

## Tips

- Changes only save once you select **Save**; **Cancel** leaves your details unchanged.
- The **Recovery** tab is where you will find auto-saved drafts from the Compose editor.
- Set your preferred light or dark theme from the **Settings** tab.
