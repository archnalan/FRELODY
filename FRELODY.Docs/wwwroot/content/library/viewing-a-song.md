# Viewing and playing a song

> Open a saved song to see its chords and lyrics laid out, and play along your way.

## Overview

Every song in FRELODY has its own page where you can read the chords and lyrics together and play through it.

Open a song at `/songs/{SongId}`, where `{SongId}` is the song's identifier. The page renders the song player so the chart is ready to follow.

How the song looks follows your display preference. The **Mode** setting lets you switch between the full view (lyrics, chords and chord charts together), lyrics with chords, or lyrics only — so you can keep the page as detailed or as clean as you like.

Each song also has its own settings and a rating, so you can fine-tune the view and mark the songs you come back to.

## Steps

1. From your library, tap a song to open its page at `/songs/{SongId}`.
2. Read along — the chords sit above the matching lyrics.
3. To change how much you see, set your **Mode** to full, lyrics and chords, or lyrics only.
4. Rate the song if you'd like to remember your favourites.

## Tips

- The display **Mode** is your account preference, so it carries across every song you open. See [Light and dark themes](/docs/getting-started/themes-and-display) to set it.
- Want to send a song to a friend? Use a share link.
