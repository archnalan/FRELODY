# Songbooks and categories

> Organise your songs into songbooks and group them by book, artist, album and category.

## Overview

Songbooks are a richer way to organise your music than a flat list. They collect songs together and let you sort them by book, artist, album and category.

Open them at `/songbooks`. The page is headed **Song Books** and shows a card for each songbook with its title and description, plus a **Categories** link.

Tap **Categories** (or open `/songbooks/{SongBookId}`) to drill into a single songbook and work with its categories. From here you can shape how songs are grouped using the dedicated artist, album, key and category controls.

Songbooks are a member feature, so sign in to create and manage them.

## Steps

1. Go to `/songbooks` to see your **Song Books** as cards.
2. Read each card's title and description to find the right one.
3. Select **Categories** on a songbook, or open `/songbooks/{SongBookId}`, to view its contents.
4. Group songs by book, artist, album or category using the customise controls.

## Tips

- Songbooks suit collections you keep coming back to, like a worship set or an album you're learning. For lighter, ad-hoc groupings, a playlist may be quicker.
