# Previewing a song without an account

> See what a FRELODY song page looks like before you sign in.

## Overview

You don't need an account to get a feel for how FRELODY shows a song. The preview gives signed-out visitors a look at the song view without saving anything.

Preview mode opens at `/songs/preview`. FRELODY recognises a preview when there's no specific song chosen and the address ends in `/preview`, then shows the song view in preview form.

There's also a share-friendly version at `/songs/landing/{SongId}` (and `/songs/landing/preview`). These landing pages are built to display cleanly when a song is shared, and they're where share links send visitors.

If you like what you see, creating an account unlocks the full experience: saving songs to your library, building playlists and more.

## Steps

1. Open `/songs/preview` to view a song in preview mode.
2. Look over how chords and lyrics are laid out.
3. When you're ready for the full features, create an account. See [Sign in and sign out](/docs/getting-started/sign-in-and-sign-out).

## Tips

- Followed a shared song? You'll usually land on a `/songs/landing/...` page — the same view, made for sharing.
