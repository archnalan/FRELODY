# Your songs library

> Find, search and manage every song you've saved or created, all in one place.

## Overview

Your library is the home for your music. Every song you save from **Discover** or build in **Compose** lands here, ready to open, play, edit or organise.

Open it at `/songs-library`. The page is headed **Library** and gives you a search box and an **Add a Song** button so you can jump straight into a new chart.

There is also a read-only listing at `/songs`. It shows the same songs but hides the search box and actions, so it's handy for browsing without changing anything.

When you have a lot of songs, the list loads smoothly as you scroll, so even a big collection stays quick to move through.

## Steps

1. Go to `/songs-library`. You'll see the **Library** heading with your songs listed.
2. Type into the **Search...** box to filter by title and find a song fast.
3. Select **Add a Song** to start a new chart in the editor.
4. Tap any song to open it and start playing.
5. To act on several songs at once, switch into selection mode, tick the songs you want, then use the bulk actions in the toast that appears.

## Tips

- Use `/songs` when you just want to browse a tidy, read-only view.
- Saving a song from **Discover** adds it here automatically — no extra step needed.
