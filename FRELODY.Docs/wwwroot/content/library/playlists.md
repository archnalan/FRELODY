# Playlists and folders

> Group your songs into playlists and folders so the right set is always a tap away.

## Overview

Playlists let you gather songs into sets — a gig list, a practice batch, or just a bundle of favourites.

Open them at `/playlists`. The page is headed **Playlists**, with a **Search playlists...** box and a **New** button to create one. Your own sets sit under **My Playlists**.

You can keep things tidy with folders, which group related playlists together, and each playlist holds its song items. Open a single playlist's detail view at `/playlists/{PlaylistId}/detail` to see and manage what's inside.

When you share a playlist, visitors see a public version at the `/playlists/landing/...` routes, so they can view your set without an account.

## Steps

1. Go to `/playlists`. You'll see the **Playlists** heading and your sets under **My Playlists**.
2. Select **New** to create a playlist.
3. Add songs to it, searching from the song picker.
4. Use folders to group related playlists together.
5. Open a playlist at `/playlists/{PlaylistId}/detail` to review or rearrange its songs.

## Tips

- Use the **Search playlists...** box to jump to a set quickly once you have a few.
- Saving songs into playlists is a member feature — make sure you're signed in. See [Sign in and sign out](/docs/getting-started/sign-in-and-sign-out).
