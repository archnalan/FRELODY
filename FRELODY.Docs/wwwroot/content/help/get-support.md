# Get support

> Find help, send feedback and learn what priority support adds on premium plans.

## Overview

If something is not working as expected, or you simply want to tell us what would make FRELODY better, you have a couple of ways to reach us. Many questions are already answered in the [Frequently asked questions](/docs/help/faq) and the [Glossary](/docs/help/glossary), so it is worth a quick look there first.

The fastest way to reach the team from inside the app is the feedback dialog, where you can send a message, report a problem or share a suggestion.

## How to get help

1. Check the [Frequently asked questions](/docs/help/faq) for a quick answer.
2. Open the in-app feedback dialog to send a message, report a bug or suggest an idea.
3. For billing questions, see [Plans and billing](/docs/pricing/plans).

## Priority support

Premium plans include priority support, so your messages are handled faster when you need a hand. You can compare what each plan includes on the [Plans and billing](/docs/pricing/plans) page.

## Tips

- Include as much detail as you can, such as the song link or page you were on, so we can help you sooner.
