# Frequently asked questions

> Quick answers to the questions people ask most about plans, payments and the free daily limit.

## Overview

This page collects the most common questions about using FRELODY, especially around plans, payments and the free daily limit. If your question is not here, see [Get support](/docs/help/get-support).

## Plans and billing

**Can I switch plans later?**
Yes. You can switch between Free, Monthly, Yearly and Lifetime at any time, and upgrades take effect immediately.

**Is there really a free plan?**
Yes. The **Starter (free)** plan lets you analyse two songs every day, forever, with no card required.

**How does cancellation work?**
Monthly and Yearly plans can be cancelled any time, and you keep access until the end of the period you have paid for. Lifetime never expires, so there is nothing to cancel.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
PayPal worldwide, and PesaPal for mobile money, cards and bank transfer across Africa.

**Do you offer refunds?**
Studio plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. For other plans, reach out to our support team.

## The free daily limit

**Why only two free songs a day?**
Analysing a song is real work behind the scenes, so the free tier covers two new songs each day. It keeps the free plan genuinely free while keeping the service fast for everyone.

**Do songs already in FRELODY count toward my daily limit?**
No. Only new YouTube and TikTok analyses count toward your daily limit. Songs already in the FRELODY library are free to open.

## Tips

- Songs you unlock today stay free to replay for 24 hours, listed under **Today's Songs**.
- Want to remove the daily cap and play longer songs? See [How premium access works](/docs/pricing/how-premium-works).
