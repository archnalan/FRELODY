# Organization dashboard

> Get an at-a-glance view of your organization, with quick links to members, projects and plans.

## Overview

The organization dashboard is the home of your admin area. Open `/admin/dashboard`. The header shows your organization's name and industry, an **Admin** pill, and links to **Members**, **Projects** and **Plans**.

The dashboard is gated to **Owner**, **Admin** and **Manager**. Without one of those roles you will see "You need the Owner, Admin, or Manager role inside an organization to view this page."

Below the header are metric cards that summarise activity. A brand-new organization gets a "Welcome to <org>" getting-started panel with prompts to **Invite members**, **Add a song** and **Create a playlist** — a quick way to set the workspace up.

## Steps

1. Open `/admin/dashboard` (you need the **Owner**, **Admin** or **Manager** role).
2. Review the metric cards for a snapshot of activity.
3. Use the header links to open **Members**, **Projects** or **Plans**.
4. On a new organization, follow the getting-started panel to **Invite members**, **Add a song** or **Create a playlist**.

## Tips

- Use the **Members** link to invite teammates and set their roles.
- The getting-started panel only appears while the organization is new.
