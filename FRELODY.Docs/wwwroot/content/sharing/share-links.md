# Share links

> Create a link to a song so anyone can open it, with a tidy preview card built in.

## Overview

A share link is a quick way to send a song to someone else. FRELODY copies a link to your clipboard that opens the song for whoever you send it to.

You'll find it in the song's share menu. Choose **Share Link** and FRELODY copies the URL, confirming with **Share link copied to clipboard!**. If a link doesn't exist yet, one is generated for you.

The same menu has a **Customize** option for editing (this is sign-in gated). If you're the owner and on **Premium**, or a SuperAdmin, you also get a visibility toggle to switch a song between **Private** and **Visible**.

Every share link carries a small preview snapshot — a title, description and image — so it looks good when posted, and it can have an optional expiry date.

## Steps

1. Open the song you want to share.
2. Open its share menu and choose **Share Link**.
3. Wait for **Share link copied to clipboard!** to confirm the copy.
4. Paste the link wherever you like — chat, email or social.
5. To control who can see it, use the **Private** / **Visible** toggle (owner and **Premium**, or SuperAdmin).

## Tips

- The preview card is generated automatically — see [Social cards (Open Graph)](/docs/sharing/social-cards) for how it looks when posted.
- Visitors who open your link land on a public page that works without an account. See [Public song and playlist pages](/docs/sharing/public-landings).
