# Plans and billing

> See what FRELODY costs, what you get for free, and what Premium unlocks.

## Overview

FRELODY turns any song into a playable chord chart. You can do plenty without paying a penny, and upgrade later if you want to play more.

There are two tiers. **Starter (free)** lets you analyse 2 songs every day, with songs up to 8 minutes long. **Premium** removes the daily cap and raises the length limit to 20 minutes. Premium comes as **Creator** (for a single player) or **Studio** (for a band, church or team).

You don't need a card to start, and you can cancel anytime. The pricing page is at `/pricing`.

## What each plan includes

- **Starter (free)** — 2 song analyses every day, songs up to 8 minutes, and the core practice tools: auto chord detection, slow-down practice, section looping and the chord timeline. Premium-only features show a small lock icon.
- **Creator (Premium)** — unlimited song analyses with no daily cap, full-length songs up to 20 minutes, **Today's Songs**, chord-chart printing and clean PDF export. Carries the **Most popular** badge.
- **Studio (Premium)** — everything in Creator plus a shared team library and priority support, aimed at a band, church or team.

## Choosing how you're billed

On the pricing page, use the billing toggle to switch between **Monthly**, **Yearly (−20%)** and **Lifetime**.

1. Open `/pricing`.
2. Pick **Monthly**, **Yearly** or **Lifetime** at the top of the cards.
3. Read the price — yearly shows a per-month figure and the total billed each year.
4. Choose **Start free** for Starter, or **Go Premium** / **Get lifetime access** for Premium.

## Tips

- Prices show in your local currency (converted from Ugandan shillings). PayPal payments settle in US dollars.
- Songs already in FRELODY's library don't count towards your daily limit — only new YouTube and TikTok analyses do.
- Lifetime never expires, so there's nothing to cancel later.
