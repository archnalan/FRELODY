# Pay with PesaPal

> Upgrade to Premium with mobile money, a card or a bank transfer through PesaPal.

## Overview

PesaPal is one of two ways to pay for Premium on FRELODY (the other is [PayPal](/docs/pricing/paypal-checkout)). It's the easiest option if you want to pay with mobile money, and it also takes cards and bank transfers across Africa.

You start a PesaPal payment from the checkout window on the pricing page. FRELODY opens PesaPal's secure gateway in a pop-up window so you can complete the payment — your details stay with PesaPal.

This page assumes you're signed in. If you're not, see [Sign in and sign out](/docs/getting-started/sign-in-and-sign-out) first.

## Steps

1. Go to `/pricing` and choose your plan and billing period.
2. Select **Go Premium** (or **Get lifetime access**) to open the **Complete your upgrade** window.
3. Choose the **Mobile / Card** tab. The fineprint reads "Secured by PesaPal — mobile money, cards & bank transfer."
4. Select **Continue with Mobile Money / Card**.
5. The **Complete Your Payment** window opens with the PesaPal gateway inside it.
6. Choose mobile money, card or bank transfer and follow PesaPal's prompts.
7. When PesaPal confirms, you're returned to FRELODY with Premium active.

## Tips

- Have your phone nearby for mobile money — you may need to approve a prompt on your device.
- Don't close the **Complete Your Payment** window until PesaPal finishes; closing early can interrupt the payment.
- Prices show in your local currency, converted from Ugandan shillings.
- Premium is granted as soon as your payment is captured, so there's nothing else to switch on.
