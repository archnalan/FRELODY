# Managing your subscription

> Understand how to switch plans, cancel, or check your Premium status.

## Overview

This page is for members who already have Premium and want to change or check it.

FRELODY doesn't have a dedicated self-serve "manage subscription" screen. Instead, you manage your plan through the same pricing flow you used to upgrade, and your current state is reflected by your billing status. The points below come straight from FRELODY's pricing FAQ and billing behaviour.

You can move between **Starter (free)**, **Monthly**, **Yearly** and **Lifetime** at any time, and upgrades take effect immediately.

## Switching plans

1. Go to `/pricing`.
2. Pick the billing period you want — **Monthly**, **Yearly** or **Lifetime**.
3. Choose **Go Premium** or **Get lifetime access** and complete checkout.
4. The change takes effect immediately.

## Cancelling

- **Monthly** and **Yearly** plans can be cancelled anytime. You keep Premium access until the end of the period you've already paid for, then your account returns to the free limits.
- **Lifetime** never expires, so there's nothing to cancel.

## Checking your status

You're Premium while your billing status is a trial, an active recurring plan, or a lifetime plan that hasn't expired. If Premium features stop working, a recurring plan has most likely reached its renewal date. To talk through a change, refunds or a billing question, use the in-app feedback option or contact support.

## Tips

- Upgrades apply at once, so there's no waiting period after you pay.
- Downgrading to free won't delete your songs, playlists or songbooks — only the Premium limits change.
- Studio plans carry a 30-day money-back guarantee; for other plans, contact support about refunds.
