# How premium access works

> Learn exactly what Premium unlocks and how FRELODY decides you're a Premium member.

## Overview

Most people on FRELODY hit the free limit around their third song — that's usually when Premium starts to make sense. This page explains what changes the moment you upgrade.

On the free plan you can analyse 2 songs each day, and each song can be up to 8 minutes long. Songs you unlock stay playable for 24 hours. Premium lifts those limits and switches on the rest of FRELODY's features.

Premium is granted server-side the moment your payment is captured, so access turns on as soon as you pay — there's nothing to enter or activate by hand.

## What Premium unlocks

- **Unlimited song analyses** — no daily cap, so you can keep pasting links all day.
- **Full-length songs, up to 20 minutes** instead of 8.
- **Today's Songs** — your 24-hour list of unlocked songs, ready to replay.
- **Chord-chart printing** and clean **PDF export**.
- The full data-driven feature set shown on the plan cards (such as a shared team library and priority support on **Studio**).

## How FRELODY knows you're Premium

FRELODY checks your billing status. You count as Premium when your account is on a trial, an active recurring plan, or a lifetime plan — and hasn't expired.

- **Monthly** and **Yearly** plans renew on a cycle; if a plan lapses, access drops back to the free limits.
- **Lifetime** never expires, so it stays on permanently.
- Platform administrators always have Premium access.

## Tips

- Upgrades take effect immediately — you don't need to sign out and back in.
- If you ever lose Premium features unexpectedly, check that your plan hasn't reached its renewal date.
