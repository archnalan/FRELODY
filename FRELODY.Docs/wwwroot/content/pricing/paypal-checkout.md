# Pay with PayPal

> Upgrade to Premium with PayPal, charged securely in US dollars.

## Overview

PayPal is one of two ways to pay for Premium on FRELODY (the other is [PesaPal](/docs/pricing/pesapal-checkout)). It's handy if you already have a PayPal account, and it works worldwide.

You start a PayPal payment from the checkout window on the pricing page. PayPal payments are charged in US dollars, and you confirm everything inside PayPal's own secure window — FRELODY never sees your card details.

This page assumes you're signed in. If you're not, see [Sign in and sign out](/docs/getting-started/sign-in-and-sign-out) first.

## Steps

1. Go to `/pricing` and choose your plan and billing period.
2. Select **Go Premium** (or **Get lifetime access**) to open the **Complete your upgrade** window.
3. Choose the **PayPal** tab. The fineprint reads "Charged in USD. You'll confirm securely in PayPal."
4. Wait for the PayPal buttons to load — you'll briefly see "Loading PayPal…".
5. Select the PayPal button and confirm the payment in PayPal's window.
6. FRELODY shows "Confirming your payment…", then "You're Premium! 🎉".
7. The page reloads into **Discover** with your new limits already active.

## Tips

- If the PayPal tab shows a **Soon** badge or "PayPal isn't available right now", PayPal isn't switched on for your region yet — use **Mobile / Card** via [PesaPal](/docs/pricing/pesapal-checkout) instead.
- Premium is granted the instant your payment is captured, so there's nothing else to activate.
- Prices on the pricing page show in your local currency, but PayPal settles the charge in US dollars.
