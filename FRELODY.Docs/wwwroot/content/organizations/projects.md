# Projects

> See all the content your organization has created — songs, playlists and songbooks — in one place.

## Overview

The **Projects** page gathers everything created inside your organization. Open `/admin/projects`, titled "Projects — All content created under <org>."

The page is organised into tabs for **Songs**, **Playlists** and **Songbooks**. Each tab links through to the matching part of the app (`/songs`, `/playlists` and `/songbooks`), so you can move from an overview of the workspace into the content itself.

This page is for organization staff: it is gated to **Owner**, **Admin**, **Manager** and **Editor**. Use it as a quick map of what the team has built.

## Steps

1. Open `/admin/projects` (you need the **Owner**, **Admin**, **Manager** or **Editor** role).
2. Select the **Songs**, **Playlists** or **Songbooks** tab.
3. Follow a tab's link to open that content in the app.

## Tips

- Projects is a launch pad — the content lives in the standard library sections it links to.
- If you cannot open this page, ask an Owner or Admin to grant you an Editor role or higher.
