# Join an organization

> Switch into a team's workspace using the organization ID from your invite.

## Overview

Joining an organization makes it your active workspace, so the songs, playlists and songbooks you see are the team's. You join with an **Organization ID** that the owner shares with you.

Open `/organizations/join`. The page is titled "Join an organization" with the subtitle "Switch your active workspace." Paste the ID into the **Organization ID** field — the help text reads "Ask the org owner for the ID, or open the invite link they sent."

When you select **Continue**, FRELODY previews the switch and asks you to confirm before changing your active workspace. This is so you do not switch away from your current organization by accident.

## Steps

1. Ask the organization's owner for the **Organization ID**, or open the invite link they sent.
2. Open `/organizations/join`.
3. Paste the ID into the **Organization ID** field.
4. Select **Continue** to preview the switch.
5. Confirm in the prompt to make this organization your active workspace.

## Tips

- Switching workspaces changes which content you see; you can switch back the same way.
- If the owner sent an invite link, opening it can carry the ID for you.
