# Create an organization

> Set up a shared workspace for your team's music in a couple of fields.

## Overview

Creating an organization gives your band, church or group a shared workspace for songs, playlists and songbooks. As the person who creates it, you become its **Owner**.

Open `/organizations/create`. The page is titled "Create your organization" with the subtitle "A workspace for your team's music." You only need a few details to get going.

If you are already a member of an organization, FRELODY will tell you: "You're already a member of <Name>. Leave it first if you want to create a new one." A **View my organization** link takes you to your current workspace.

## Steps

1. Open `/organizations/create`.
2. Enter an **Organization name** (for example, "Riverside Worship Band"). This field is required.
3. Choose an **Industry**.
4. Choose a **Country**.
5. Submit the form to create the organization and become its Owner.

## Tips

- You can only belong to one organization at a time; leave your current one before creating a new one.
- Pick a clear, recognisable name — your team will see it when they join.
