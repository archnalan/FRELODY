# Create an account

> Set up a free FRELODY account so you can save songs, build charts and pick up where you left off.

## Overview

You can try FRELODY without an account, but creating one lets you save analysed songs to your library, use the **Compose** editor, build playlists and songbooks, and keep your settings between visits. It is free to start, and no card is needed.

Sign-up is a short, guided wizard with a step indicator at the top so you always know where you are. You will enter your name and email, then confirm your email with a one-time code we send you.

## Steps

1. Go to `/register`, or open the sign-up link from the sign-in screen or the pricing page.
2. On the **Create your FRELODY account** step, enter your full name and email address.
3. Continue through the wizard, following the step indicator to the end.
4. When prompted, enter the one-time verification code sent to your email to confirm your address.
5. Finish the wizard to land in FRELODY, ready to analyse and save songs.

## Tips

- If you started sign-up from the pricing page or another flow, you will be returned there once your account is ready, so you do not lose your place.
- Already have an account? Head to [Sign in and sign out](/docs/getting-started/sign-in-and-sign-out) instead.
- Check your spam folder if the verification email does not arrive within a minute.
