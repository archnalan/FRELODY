# Sign in and sign out

> Get back into your FRELODY account and sign out safely when you are done.

## Overview

Signing in unlocks your saved songs, the **Compose** editor, your playlists and your settings. The sign-in card is headed **Welcome back** and you can reach it at `/login`.

You can sign in with your email or username and password, or use **Continue with Google**. If you have visited before, FRELODY may show a **Continue as** chip so you can pick up your session in one tap, reviving it quietly in the background where possible.

## Steps

1. Go to `/login`.
2. Enter your details in **Email or Username** and your password, or choose **Continue with Google**.
3. Submit to sign in. If you arrived from another page, you will be returned there afterwards.
4. To sign out, open your account menu and choose to sign out.

## Tips

- New to FRELODY? Use the sign-up toggle on the sign-in screen, or see [Create an account](/docs/getting-started/create-an-account).
- If you are asked to set a new password before continuing, this is a one-time security step at `/account/change-password-required`.
- Forgotten your password? Use the password-reset flow described in [Password and security](/docs/account/password-and-security).
