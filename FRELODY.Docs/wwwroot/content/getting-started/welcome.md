# Welcome to FRELODY

> Meet FRELODY, the quickest way to go from a song you just heard to chords you can actually play.

## Overview

FRELODY turns any song into a playable chord chart. You paste a YouTube or TikTok link, FRELODY detects the chords and lines them up with the audio, and then you slow the tempo down and loop the tricky bars until you can play along.

It is built for hobbyist musicians who want to learn a song fast, without hunting through forums or squinting at a blurry tab. The whole experience is designed around one simple loop: **hear it, slow it down, play it.**

You can start straight away. The home page is the **Discover** search, so there is no splash screen to click through and no card needed to try it. Start free, and upgrade when you are hooked.

## What you can do

- **Analyse a link.** Drop in a YouTube or TikTok song and get a synced chord chart in seconds.
- **Practise at your pace.** Slow the song down and loop the hard sections until they stick.
- **Build your own charts.** Write and edit songs in the **Compose** editor.
- **Organise everything.** Keep your songs in a library, group them into playlists and songbooks, then print or share your work.

## Tips

- Signed-out visitors can preview songs and try analysis within the free daily limit, so you can explore before creating an account.
- Going further (saving songs, building charts, sharing) only takes a free account.
