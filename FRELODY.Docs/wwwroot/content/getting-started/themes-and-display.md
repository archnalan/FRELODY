# Light and dark themes

> Switch between light and dark themes, and choose how much of a song you want to see.

## Overview

FRELODY comes in light and dark themes so you can match your room or your mood. You can flip between them any time using the theme toggle in the navigation bar. Your choice is remembered, so FRELODY opens the way you left it.

Signed-in members get a couple of extra display options under **Settings**, including a per-account theme preference and a control for how much detail each song shows.

## Steps

1. Find the theme toggle in the navigation bar.
2. Tap it to switch between light and dark. FRELODY applies the change instantly and remembers it for next time.
3. For more options, open **Settings** from your account.
4. Under **Appearance**, pick a **Theme**, and under **Display**, choose a song **Mode**.

## Display modes

The song **Mode** segmented control changes how much of a song you see:

- **Full** — lyrics, chords and chord charts together.
- **Lyrics and chords** — words with chords above, no charts.
- **Lyrics only** — just the words.

You can also turn the **Notify** switch on or off in **Settings**.

## Tips

- The theme follows your in-app choice, not your device's system setting, so what you pick in FRELODY is what you get.
- Pick **Lyrics only** for clean, distraction-free singing, or **Full** when you are learning the shapes.
